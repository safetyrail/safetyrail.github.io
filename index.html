Safety Rail App

Protect your loved ones from frauds and scams.

Welcome to Safety Rail - your family's digital guardian against online fraud and scams. According to the FBI, $10.3 Billion was lost to Online Fraud in 2022, solely in the USA. Safety Rail App is here to make a difference.

We're building a mobile app that helps your family understand threats and stay safe online. Some of the most common online risks are frauds like:

  • Phishing: Attacker tricking you with familiar-looking messages, aiming to steal sensitive info like passwords or credit card numbers.
  • Tech support scams: Fraudsters pretending to be from tech support to gain access to your computer.
  • Romance scam: Scammers pretending to be interested in a romantic relationship to gain your trust, then ask for money.

Many of the scams are easily detected by tech-savvy individuals, but the older generation, like our parents, is at a higher risk. Out of the $10.3 billion lost last year due to online fraud, 30% was scammed from elderly people.
